About Campus Connect

Your comprehensive platform for college event management

Our Mission

To streamline college event management and enhance student participation through a unified digital platform.

Campus Connect was developed by students of MIT Academy of Engineering to address the challenges of event management in educational institutions. Our platform provides a seamless experience for event organizers, participants, and administrators.

We believe that well-organized events contribute significantly to the holistic development of students, and our mission is to make event participation and management as smooth as possible.

Key Features

Event Management

Comprehensive tools for creating, promoting, and managing college events of all types and scales.

QR-Based Attendance

Efficient attendance tracking using QR codes, eliminating manual processes and reducing errors.

Analytics & Insights

Detailed reports and analytics to measure event success and improve future planning.
