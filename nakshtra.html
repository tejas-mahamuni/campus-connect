About Nakshatra

Nakshatra is MIT Academy of Engineering's flagship cultural festival, bringing together talent from across the country. Join us for three days of music, dance, drama, and more!

With over 20 events across various categories, Nakshatra offers a platform for students to showcase their talents and compete with peers from different colleges. Whether you're a performer or a spectator, Nakshatra promises an unforgettable experience.

Event Categories

Dance

Solo and group dance competitions across various styles including classical, contemporary, and folk.

Audition Information

Auditions for Nakshatra 2025 will be held from January 15-20, 2025. All participants must register online before attending auditions.

  • Dance auditions: January 15-16
  • Music auditions: January 17-18
  • Drama auditions: January 19-20

Selected participants will be notified by January 25, 2025.
